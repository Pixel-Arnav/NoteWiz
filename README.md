# NoteWiz - Capture and Organize Your Notes Effortlessly

**NoteWiz** is your go-to extension for quickly capturing, organizing, and managing notes while browsing. Take control of your thoughts, ideas, and tasks without leaving your browser window!

## Features
- **Instant Note Saving**: Quickly jot down ideas or important information with a sleek, user-friendly interface.
- **Categories & Organization**: Categorize your notes into work, personal, or to-do lists, and stay on top of your tasks.
- **Pin & Edit Notes**: Pin important notes to the top of your list and edit them effortlessly whenever needed.
- **Search Notes**: Easily find notes using the integrated search functionality.
- **Dark Mode Toggle**: Switch between light and dark modes for a personalized note-taking experience.
- **Sync Across Devices**: Notes sync across devices using Chrome storage, so you can access them anywhere.

From brainstorming to-do lists to storing quick reminders, NoteWiz keeps your notes neatly organized and accessible from anywhere.
